KSP Fact Check
Director General & Inspector General of Police

Shri. Praveen Sood, IPS

    It indeed is a great honour and privilege for me to lead one lakh strong state police force for serving 6.5 crore people of Karnataka. Karnataka State Police has been pioneer in leveraging technology at least a decade before rest of the country. Be it CCTNS,Cyber Crime Police Station, in house state of art Police Data Centre or Automated Fingerprints Identification System (AFIS); we have been the leaders.On the welfare front, Arogya Bhagya for the health of policemen and their families has been replicated by many other states. We are the pioneers in starting absolutely transparent and objective system for the recruitment of constabulary and Sub-Inspectors. The next step is to integrate CCTNS with the courts and prisons under the aegis of ICJS.

    In addition to maintenance of law & order, communal harmony and prevention of crime, our top priority is to take above technological initiatives to the next level, with focus on cyber crime, crime against women and children and menace of drugs.

    Soon we would launch Seva Sindhu i.e., delivering 18 services including all types of police verifications electronically without any leg work or physical contact with police, whether at the application stage or at the stage of receiving the certificate.

    Strengthening of Forensic Science Laboratory with special focus on Cyber Forensic, DNA testing and Finger Print Bureau would be done on priority.

    However,no police force can deliver without active involvement of citizens. With your support and constructive criticism, we will leave no stone unturned to make the state safe and secure place for everyone. Look forward to your valuable cooperation in all our endeavours.

    sd/-
    ( Praveen Sood, IPS )
    Director General & Inspector General of Police,
    Karnataka State
